Outlook

The outlook for lithium-ion batteries (LIBs) remains highly promising, driven by escalating demands for renewable energy storage and electric vehicles (EVs). The global LIB market is projected to grow significantly, with an anticipated compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of over 15% from 2023 to 2030, primarily driven by the EV industry's expansion and grid-scale energy storage solutions (BloombergNEF, 2023).
